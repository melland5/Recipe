Grilled Cheese

Decription

A delicouse toasted sandwich wih cheese

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Take skiled and put butter in it.
  2. Put skilled on cooker and set to medium
  3. When butter is melted put bread on in the skillet
  4. When bread absorbs all of the butter put your cheese in between of the two slices of bread
    5. Once cheese is melted and bread is toasted take of the skillet and enjoy!
